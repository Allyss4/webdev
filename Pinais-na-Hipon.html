Pinais na Hipon

Dish Recipes:


  • ½ pound Shrimp
  • ¼ cup Shrimp juice
  • ¼ pound Pork belly, chopped
  • 2 cups Young coconut, shredded
  • 1 Onion, peeled and chopped
  • 4 Garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups Coconut cream
  • Banana leaves

Instructions:


  1. Rinse banana leaves well, and wipe them down to dry to rid off any dirt or debris. Cut into about 6-inch squares.

  2. Peel, devein and cut shrimp into small pieces. Reserve shrimp heads.

  3. In a bowl, combine ¼ cup water and shrimp heads. With the back of a spoon, gently press heads to extract the juice. Using a fine-mesh sieve, strain and set aside juice.

  4. In a bowl, combine shrimp, pork, shredded young coconut, onions, garlic, and shrimp juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. On a banana leaf, spoon about 3 tablespoons of shrimp mixture. Fold sides of the leaf over filling to form a packet and tie with kitchen twine to fully secure.

  6. In a wide pan, arrange banana packets in a single layer and pour coconut cream.

  7. Cook over medium heat for about 15 to 20 minutes. Transfer banana packets onto a serving platter and keep warm.

  8. Simmer coconut sauce in the pan until slightly reduced and thickened. Serve in a bowl on the side to spoon on the pinais.
