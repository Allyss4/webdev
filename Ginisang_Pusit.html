Ginisang Pusit

Dish Recipes:


  • 1 lb. squid (cleaned and sliced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced diagonally)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced into squared)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced into squares)
  • 2 stalks leeks (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 thumb ginger (minced)
  • 1 ½ tablespoons cooking wine
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons baking soda

Sauce Ingredients:


  • 4 grams Maggi Magic Sarap
  • 3 ½ tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

Instructions:


  1. Combine the squid and baking soda in a bowl. Mix well. Let it stay for 15 to 30 minutes. Rinse the squid and drain the water. Set aside.
  2. Combine the sauce ingredients in a bowl. Stir until well blended. Set aside.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a wok. Sauté the carrot, onion, white part of the leek, garlic, ginger, and bell peppers for 1 minute.
  4. Add the squid. Stir-fry for 1 ½ minutes.
  5. Pour the cooking wine. Toss.
  6. Add the sauce. Stir and cook for 30 seconds.
  7. Season with ground white pepper.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with rice.
  9. Share and enjoy!
