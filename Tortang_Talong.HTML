Tortang Talong

Dish Description:

Tortang Talong is a delicious Filipino omelette made with grilled eggplant (talong) that is mashed and mixed with beaten eggs, then pan-fried until golden and crispy. It is often served with rice and a side of dipping sauce, like soy sauce with calamansi or vinegar. This simple yet flavorful dish is a common comfort food, perfect for breakfast or a light meal.

Dish Recipes:


  • Chinese Eggplant
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil



Instructions:


  1. Grill the eggplant until the color of skin turns almost black.

  2. Let the eggplant cool for a while then peel off the skin. Set aside.

  3. Crack the eggs and place in a bowl.

  4. Add salt and beat.

  5. Place the eggplant on a flat surface and flatten using a fork.

  6. Dip the flattened eggplant in the beaten egg mixture.

  7. Heat the pan and pour the cooking oil

  8. Fry the eggplant (that was dipped in the beaten mixture). Make sure that both sides are cooked.
