Turon

Dish Recipes:


  • 6 pieces Bbananas saba or plantains, cut in half (lengthwise)
  • 1 cup Jackfruit ripe (Sliced)
  • 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 12 pieces Lumpia wrapper
  • 2 cups Cooking oil

Instructions:


  1. Roll the banana on the sugar plate and ensure that the banana is coated with enough sugar.
  2. 6 pieces bananas, 1 1/2 cup sugar.
  3. Place the banana with sugar coating on the lumpia wrapper. Add a slice of ripe jackfruit on top.
  4. 12 pieces lumpia wrapper, 1 cup jackfruit.
  5. Fold and lock the spring roll wrapper, and use water to seal the edge.
  6. In a pan, heat the oil and put in some sugar. Wait until the brown sugar floats in 2 cups of cooking oil.
  7. Put in the wrapped banana and fry until the wrapper turns golden brown and the extra sugar sticks to the wrapper.
  8. Serve hot as a dessert or snack. Share and Enjoy!
