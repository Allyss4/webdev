Sinangag (Garlic Fried Rice)

Dish Recipes:


  • 3 cups Cooked white rice
  • 5 cloves Crushed garlic
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons Cooking oil

Instructions:


  1. Heat the cooking oil in a wide pan.

  2. While the cooking oil is being heated, add the crushed garlic. Note: make sure that the oil is not hot when you add the garlic. Let the garlic slowly cook while the oil gets heated until it becomes golden brown and crisp.

  3. Sprinkle the salt over the rice. Toss until the salt gets distributed evenly.

  4. Gradually add the rice into the pan. Stir to distribute the ingredients. Continue to stir fry for 3 to 5 minutes.

  5. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with your favorite dish.

  6. Share and enjoy!
