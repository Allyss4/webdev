Pancit Habhab

Dish Recipes:


  • 1 lb. Habhab noodles
  • 2 sachet Knorr SavorRich Liquid Seasoning 16 gram
  • 1/2 lb. boneless chicken breast boiled and shredded
  • 1 piece chayote sliced
  • 20 pieces of snow peas
  • 3/4 cup carrots julienne
  • 2 bunches of baby bok choy chopped
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cups water or chicken stock
  • 6 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:


  1. Heat 3 tablespoons oil in a cooking pot. Saute chayote for 2 minutes. Add carrot and saute for 30 to 40 seconds. Put the snow peas into the pot. Season with salt and pepper. Add bok choy and cook for 1 minute. Remove the vegetables from the pot and place in a clean bowl. Set aside.

  2. Heat the remaining oil in the same pot. Saute onion and garlic until onion softens.

  3. Add shredded chicken. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes.

  4. Add Knorr SavorRich Chicken Liquid Seasoning. Continue to cook for 1 minute or until the chicken gets completely coated.

  5. Pour chicken stock (this is the water used to boil the chicken breast. The amount is approximately 4 cups).

  6. Pour in soy sauce. Stir. Let the liquid boil.

  7. Put half of the hab hab noodles into the pot. Let it cook until enough liquid is absorbed. Once the noodles start to soften, add the remaining noodles. Continue to cook in low heat while tossing once in a while until the liquid is fully absorbed and the noodles become soft, but not mushy.

  8. Season with ground black pepper and put the stir-fried vegetables back into the pot. Toss and cook for 2 minutes.

  9. Transfer to a serving plate. Share and enjoy!
