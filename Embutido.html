Embutido

Dish Recipes:


  • 2 lbs. ground pork
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • Â¼ cup Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 2 pieces eggs
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese grated
  • 1 piece red bell pepper minced
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup carrots minced
  • 1 cup onion minced
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups bread crumbs

Instructions:


  1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients except for the water. Mix altogether until well blended.

  2. Wrap the mixture using aluminum foil. Cut a piece of aluminum foil to 12x8 inches. Lay it flat on a table. The shiny part of the foil should face down. Scoop 1 cup of mixture and place it on the middle top part of the foil. Mold into a cylinder. Roll the foil from top to bottom. Secure both ends of the foil. Perform this step until the mixture is consumed.

  3. Boil water in a pot. Arrange the wrapped embutido in a steamer. Steam for 1 hour.

  4. Remove from the steamer. Let it cool down. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Slice into serving pieces.

  5. Transfer to a serving plate. Share and enjoy!
